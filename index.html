ABOUT US

The industrial complex used: “Cigom”, is the first white chalk production unit. The chalk is then used in the schools of Goma. This exceptional initiative was led by Dhorasso Kambale. Its vision is to help reduce the unemployment rate and contribute to the development of education. Among 4 winners of Amani festival entrepreneurship competition , the Complexe Industriel de Goma (CIGOM) project is now supplying chalk to 74 schools in Goma, only one year after its creation, and has enabled the creation of 6 jobs! Congratulations to Dhorasso Kisenge who, at only 33 years old, carried out this promising project and thank you to the support of the Great Vision Business Academy for its support!
